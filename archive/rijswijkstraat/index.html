Rijswijkstraat 175, May 24

10.09.2024г. Iris Nikolova

Three burning crosses
—spears, ablaze,
pierce the supple flesh
of the mucky
earth beneath our quavery feet,
cut through the blackened,
greasy,
slimy
sludge—
the firmament
is cut, split
by ever-burning blazes;
the putrid scourge they lay upon us,
rancid stench
of smoke, and ash,
’s cremation of a spirit,
dagger
in an arid heart
